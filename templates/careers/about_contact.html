Who We Are

Présentation officielle de l’entreprise.

Algérie Télécom leader on the Algerian telecommunications expanding market, proposes a full range of voice and data services to the personal and business customers. This position was built via a powerful innovative policy adapted to the customer expectations and targeting the new users.

Algérie Télécom is a joint-stock company with a capital stock operating on the market of networks and electronic communications services. It was enshrined by the law 2000/03 of August 05th, 2000, relative to the reorganization of the Post and Telecommunications Sector, beside the resolutions of the National Council for State Participations (CNPE) of March 1st, 2001 that established Algérie Télécom as Public Economic Company.

Therefore, by this law Algérie Télécom is a public economic company status in a legal form of a joint-stock company. With a share capital of 115.000.000.000.00 Algerian Dinars, registered in the national center of trade register on May 11th, 2002 under number 02B 0018083.
